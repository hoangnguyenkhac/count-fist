Fish Counter System Overview

State-of-the-art AI-powered solution for aquaculture management

The Fish Counter system combines cutting-edge computer vision technology with deep learning algorithms to provide accurate fish counting and monitoring capabilities. Our system can identify and count both live and dead fish, helping aquaculture facilities optimize their operations and maintain healthy fish populations.

With both image-based analysis and real-time video processing capabilities, Fish Counter adapts to your specific needs, whether you're managing a small research tank or a large commercial fish farm.

Core Technologies

Powered by cutting-edge AI and computer vision

Deep Learning

Our system uses YOLO (You Only Look Once) neural network architecture, trained on thousands of fish images to achieve high accuracy in various conditions.

Computer Vision

Advanced image processing techniques enable our system to detect and classify fish even in challenging environments with varying lighting and water clarity.

Data Analytics

Comprehensive data storage and analysis capabilities allow for tracking trends, generating reports, and optimizing your aquaculture operations.
